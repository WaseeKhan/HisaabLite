Our Journey

2025

The Idea

The first idea was simple: local pharmacies deserved software that felt practical, fast, and trustworthy at the counter. Built in India, for Indian medical stores. #MakeInIndia #VocalForLocal

2025

Research & Design

Spoke with local store owners, studied counter workflows, and shaped the first version around billing speed, stock clarity, and operator confidence.

2025

Product Development

Built core features like billing, stock tracking, GST calculations, audit visibility, and role-safe operations. Improved the product continuously with real user feedback.

2026

Launch in Mumbai

Officially launched RxArogya and onboarded the first local medical stores in Mumbai, learning from live counter usage and day-to-day staff workflows.

2026

Continuous Improvement

Learning from pharmacists and store operators, adding features they need, and making the product stronger every day.

Future

Growing Together

Planning to help medical stores across India go digital with software that stays compact, reliable, and easy to trust.
