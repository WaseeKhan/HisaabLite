Our Story

Why we started Expygen

Small pharmacies handle billing, stock, expiry dates, GST invoices, customer requests and staff operations every single day. But most tools either feel too basic, too complex, or not built around real pharmacy counters.

Expygen was created to close that gap — a modern, reliable and easy-to-use SaaS product that helps pharmacy owners run daily operations with more control, better speed and clearer business visibility.

Made for Indian medical stores

From GST billing to WhatsApp invoice sharing, Expygen is shaped around the actual needs of Indian retail pharmacies.
