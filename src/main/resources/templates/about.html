About Expygen

Built for India's medical stores, not generic retail software

Expygen is designed around how pharmacies actually run: faster billing, cleaner inward entry, batch and expiry control, role-safe operations, and a separate Insights layer for owners and managers.

What we believe software for stores should do

Expygen is shaped by practical counter work, not decorative feature lists.

Built around the counter

Billing, purchases, and stock control should feel fast and dependable during real store hours.

Compact but capable

Powerful operations software should stay easy for owners, managers, and cashiers to understand.

Made in India

Built for Indian pharmacies by people who care about local store realities, GST flow, and practical day-to-day usage.

Why stores choose a pharmacy-first system

Expygen is built to reduce daily friction at the counter and in the stock room, while still giving owners the visibility they need.

Faster daily operations

Billing, purchases, stock adjustments, and invoice actions stay in one connected flow instead of scattered tools.

Safer role control

Owner, manager, and cashier access stays separated so the right people can operate without exposing sensitive controls.

Operate first, understand later

The main app stays operational and clean, while Insights handles sales, stock risk, purchase visibility, and audit intelligence.

Our Journey

2025

The Idea

The first idea was simple: local pharmacies deserved software that felt practical, fast, and trustworthy at the counter, not generic business software forced into medical store workflows.

2025

Research & Design

Spoke with local store owners, studied counter workflows, and shaped the first version around billing speed, stock clarity, and operator confidence.

2025

Product Development

Built core features like billing, stock tracking, GST calculations, purchase control, role-safe operations, and Insights, then kept improving with real usage feedback.

2026

Launch in Mumbai

Officially launched Expygen and onboarded local medical stores in Mumbai, learning from live counter usage and day-to-day staff workflows.

2026

Continuous Improvement

Learning from pharmacists and store operators, adding features they need, and making the product stronger every day.

Future

Growing Together

Planning to help medical stores across India go digital with software that stays compact, reliable, role-aware, and easy to trust.
