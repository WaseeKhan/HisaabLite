Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?
Yes! You can upgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately and we'll prorate the difference.
Is there a free trial for paid plans?
Yes! Enjoy a 7-day free trial on any paid plan — no credit card required. Experience all premium features risk-free. After the trial, choose the plan that works for you. Love it? Continue seamlessly. Not satisfied? Cancel with no charges.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, and bank transfers for annual plans.
Can I cancel anytime?
We don't offer cancellations during the active subscription period. Your plan continues until the end of the billing cycle. Need help or want to explore other options? Contact our support team — we're happy to assist you.
Do you offer GST invoices?
Yes, all paid plans include GST-compliant invoices with your business details.
What is your billing cycle?
We offer annual billing only. You pay once for the entire year and get uninterrupted access to your chosen plan. No monthly subscriptions, no hidden charges. Simple, straightforward, and hassle-free.
Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees ever! Our team will help you get started — completely free. From onboarding to training, the RxArogya team is here to ensure a smooth setup. Just pay for your subscription and we'll handle the rest.
