Frequently asked questions

Clear answers around plan changes, billing cycle, onboarding, and activation.

Can I switch plans later?
Yes. Stores can start small and move to larger plans later as user count, product volume, purchases, or Insights needs grow.
Is there a paid-plan trial?
You can start with the free plan and move into paid capability when your store is ready for higher limits and operational depth.
How does activation work today?
Paid activation currently follows the assisted workflow shown on the workflow page. The team confirms payment and activates the workspace.
Do all plans support GST invoicing?
Yes. GST invoicing is part of the product flow across plans, with higher plans adding more operational depth around purchases, WhatsApp delivery, and Insights.
Can I see plan usage inside the app?
Yes. Owners can view subscription status, product usage, staff usage, and plan comparison from the subscription workspace.
Which plan is best for Insights?
Premium and Enterprise are the right choices if you want the dedicated Insights workspace for sales, purchases, stock risk, and audit visibility.
